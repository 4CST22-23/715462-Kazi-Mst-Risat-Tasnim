PREVENTION OF POLLUTION


There are some ways to prevent pollution and safe our Earth.these are:

  1. Plants tress! They provide shade, clean air, mask noice and need much less water than lawns.
  2. Minimize the use of pesticides, herbicides,fertilizers. DO NOT dispose of these chemicals,moto oil,or other automotive fluids into the sanitary sewer systems. Both of them end at the river.
  3. Industry and transport areas can be separated into zones, residential areas may be made at least 20 meters away from the main streets and the space be thicky planed.
  4. Heavy vehicles should not be allowed to use narrow streets and the use of horns and pressure horns be prohibited.
  5. Raising public awarness about the effects of noice pollution.

According to the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), pollution prevention (P2) means reducing or eliminating sources of pollution to prevent damage to the environment, while also eliminating the need for costly controls and cleanup. This approach is fundamentally different and more desirable than recycling, treatment, and disposal. Key Principles: Source Reduction: Modify production processes, use less-toxic substances, and adopt better conservation techniques to reduce waste generation. Efficient Resource Use: Conserve natural resources, including water, energy, and raw materials, to minimize pollution. Best Management Practices: Implement effective practices and procedures to reduce pollution at its source. Examples of Pollution Prevention: Reducing packaging waste by optimizing product design and minimizing material usage Implementing energy-efficient technologies to reduce greenhouse gas emissions Using alternative fuels or cleaner energy sources Implementing recycling programs to conserve natural resources Reducing water pollution by implementing efficient irrigation systems and minimizing chemical use Benefits of Pollution Prevention: Cost savings through reduced waste generation and energy consumption Protection of human health and the environment Conservation of natural resources Compliance with environmental regulations and standards Pollution Prevention in Daily Life: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle in daily activities Choose products with minimal packaging and sustainable materials Conserve water and energy at home and in the workplace Support businesses and organizations that prioritize pollution prevention Pollution Prevention Hierarchy: Reduce waste generation at the source Reuse materials whenever possible Recycle materials that cannot be reused Treat or dispose of waste as a last resort By adopting pollution prevention strategies and practices, we can reduce waste, conserve resources, and protect the environment for future generations.
