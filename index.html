POLLUTION AND POLLUTION CONTROL

Pollution is the introduction of contaminants into the natural environment that cause adverse change.Pollution can take the form of chemical substance or energy,such as noice,heat or light.Pollutants, the the components of pollution, can be either foreign substances/energies or naturally occuring contaminants. Pollution is often classed as point source or nonpoint sourse polltion.
